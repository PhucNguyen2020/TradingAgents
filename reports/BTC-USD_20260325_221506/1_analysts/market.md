- **Short‑Term Bias:** The price > 10‑EMA > 50‑SMA alignment, coupled with a rising MACD and neutral RSI, supports a **bullish short‑to‑medium‑term outlook**. The bounce from the February low appears to be gaining traction.

- **Risk Management:** ATR of ~2,900 (≈4% of price) suggests a reasonable stop‑loss distance for a swing trade. Given declining volatility, tighter stops may be warranted as the market steadies.  
- **Potential Scenarios:**
