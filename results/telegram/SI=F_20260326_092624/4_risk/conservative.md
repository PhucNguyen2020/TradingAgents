As a conservative risk analyst, my job isn’t to capture every possible upside — it’s to avoid catastrophic drawdowns and ensure the portfolio can withstand stress. Silver’s 52-week range of nearly $93 shows it can drop hard and fast. Buying now, without waiting for confirmation of resilience above key technical levels, exposes the firm to unnecessary volatility that could disrupt long-term compounding.

A HOLD isn’t hesitation — it’s discipline. It’s waiting for the market to prove it can sustain higher prices before committing capital. The trend may be up, but the path is still rocky — and conservative investing means respecting the rocks, not pretending they aren’t there.
