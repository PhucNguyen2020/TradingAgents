### 4. Risk Factors  

1. **Sovereign Selling Pressure:** Continued liquidation by Bhutan or other state‑held Bitcoin could overwhelm demand, especially if ETF inflows slow.
2. **Regulatory Shock:** While the CFTC task force is innovative, future enforcement actions (e.g., on custodians, exchanges, or DeFi) could impair market access.
3. **Macro‑Correlation Breakdown:** If Bitcoin’s correlation with risk assets (e.g., Nasdaq) strengthens during a recession‑like environment, it could suffer alongside equities.
4. **Leverage Liquidations:** High leverage in futures markets remains a source of cascading liquidations during sharp moves.
5. **Custodial & Centralization Concerns:** Concentration of Bitcoin in regulated custodians (e.g., Coinbase, BlackRock) may increase systemic risk and conflict with the ethos of decentralization, potentially affecting long‑term demand from purist users.
